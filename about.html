Our history and Our Story

Clear Glazing Corporation

Clear Glazing Corporation was established in 2007 to cater to the growing demand for professional glass installers in the construction industry. With the full support of Asahi Glass Phil’s. (AGP), Clear Glazing has grown into one of the leading GLASS installers in the country. with projects from the residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality glass installation services in the construction industry, with a focus on architectural glass and aluminum, glacade and curtain wall, specialty glass, glass etching, beveling and processing. We are committed to delivering exceptional customer service and building lasting relationships with our clients.
